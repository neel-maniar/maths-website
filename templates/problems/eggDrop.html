Egg Drop

Question

You are standing on a \(100\) storey building with \(2\) eggs. These eggs are special: they only break at a certain floor (or higher!). What is the least number of drops you need to figure out which floor that is? They may not break on any of the floors!

Hint 1

You may reuse an unbroken egg.

Hint 2

What if you only had one egg?

Hint 3

What if you had infinite eggs?

Follow-up Question

Now suppose you have \(k\) eggs and \(n\) floors: how many drops would you need? You may need a computer for this one. Take a guess for how many drops you need if you have a six-pack of eggs and 1 million floors...
